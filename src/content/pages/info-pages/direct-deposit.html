Direct Deposit

It’s your money. Why wait for it? Forego the hassle of waiting for deposited paychecks or other recurring payments to clear. Direct deposit puts money in your hands faster:

  • Forget about waiting for checks to clear! With direct deposit, you can count on your funds being there when you need them in addition to same-day access!
  • Out of town? No problem! Direct deposits occur automatically.
  • Gain peace of mind knowing your paycheck won’t be misplaced or stolen! Direct deposit takes paper out of the equation. It’s entirely electronic and automated.
  • You can use direct deposit for all your recurring payments, including salary, pension, Social Security and Supplemental Security Income (SSI) benefits, and other regular monthly income.
  • Direct deposit is free, convenient, and reliable.
