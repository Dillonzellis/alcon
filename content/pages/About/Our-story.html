Banking for Life

We proudly serve all employees, retirees and immediate family members of Alcon and Novartis

In 1972, employees of Alcon gathered their funds to establish our credit union as a member-owned financial cooperative aimed at helping members succeed in life through savings and borrowing opportunities.

Fast forward to today

…And we still operate with the same goal of improving the financial lives of members…only we can now do so through a full suite of convenient products and services. Today, we offer members financial solutions for every major milestone and through every stage of life.Today, we offer banking for life.
