Courtesy Pay

We've got you covered!

  • Available to Convenience Checking account holders to cover overdraft amounts up to $500* — this limit includes all applicable fees as well as a $35 fee charged for each item presented. The overdraft limit applies to paid items; members’ balances may be greater if additional fees post to the account.
  • We reserve the right not to pay for circumstances including, but not limited to, if the account is not in good standing or if a member is not making regular deposits or has incurred an excessive number of overdrafts in the past.
  • We may authorize and/or pay overdrafts for the following types of transactions:
    • Checks and other electronic transactions made using your checking account number
    • Automatic bill payments
  • Account must be brought to a positive balance within a 30-day period. All deposits to the account are first applied to the negative balance.
